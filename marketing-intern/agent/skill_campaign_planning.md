# Skill: Campaign Planning

When preparing campaign content, always identify:

1. Campaign Goal
- What are we promoting?
- Why should people care?

2. Audience
- Who is this campaign for?
- What kind of messaging will attract them?

3. Key Message
- What is the main message?
- What should people remember?

4. Call To Action
- Register now
- Join us
- Learn more
- Attend the event

5. Deliverables
Generate:
- campaign summary
- key message
- promotional caption
- suggested tagline
- poster headline and supporting text

Keep outputs practical and usable.
