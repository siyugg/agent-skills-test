# Campaign Summary

## Campaign
**OpenShift AI Healthcare Innovation Workshop**

## Purpose
Promote a workshop that shows how AI can improve healthcare workflows, patient support, and decision assistance using a secure, scalable platform approach.

## Event Focus
The campaign positions the workshop as a practical, business-relevant session for people who want real healthcare AI use cases rather than hype. It highlights platform readiness, agentic AI use cases, live demonstrations, and the value of attending in person.

## Recommended Audience Framing
The `target_audience.md` file contains playful placeholder audience entries that do not align with the campaign brief. For usable campaign output, the messaging is aligned to the core audience named in the brief:
- healthcare professionals
- IT leaders
- technical teams
- innovation stakeholders exploring healthcare AI

## Key Message
See how OpenShift AI can help teams move from healthcare AI ideas to real-world outcomes with practical use cases, secure deployment foundations, and a live demo.

## Value Proposition
Attendees will learn:
- why AI matters in healthcare now
- how agentic AI can support healthcare use cases
- what a modern AI platform can enable
- how to turn experimentation into operational outcomes

## Call to Action
**Register now to secure your slot.**

## Creative Direction Summary
Use a clean, modern healthcare-tech look with strong hierarchy, deep blue and white as the base, soft cyan accents, and polished professional visuals suitable for poster and social media use.
