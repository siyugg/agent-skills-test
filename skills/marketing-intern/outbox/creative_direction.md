# Creative Direction

## Overall Look and Feel
Create a polished healthcare-technology visual that feels modern, professional, and trustworthy. The design should balance innovation with clarity, avoiding sci-fi exaggeration or overly clinical coldness.

## Visual Style
- Clean corporate event-poster aesthetic
- Bright, confident, professional tone
- Healthcare + AI cues without clutter
- Premium but accessible look

## Recommended Color Palette
- **Primary:** deep blue, white
- **Secondary:** soft cyan, light grey
- **Accent:** bright teal for CTA or highlight lines

## Typography Direction
- Bold sans-serif headline
- Clean sans-serif body copy
- Strong hierarchy for title, date, time, and venue
- Minimal text treatments for readability

## Layout Direction
### Poster Layout
- Top section: event title and subheadline
- Mid section: hero visual with healthcare professional(s) and subtle digital interface overlays
- Lower section: event details in a clean information block
- Bottom section: registration CTA with QR placeholder or registration note

### Social Square Layout
- Center or upper-center headline lockup
- Cropped healthcare-tech hero image
- Short supporting line
- Event date and CTA placed clearly in lower third

## Imagery Guidance
Use imagery such as:
- healthcare professionals in a modern clinical or innovation setting
- subtle AI/data interface overlays
- tablet, screen, or dashboard elements
- clean medical environment with natural lighting

## Avoid
- cartoonish styles
- childish illustration
- dark or dystopian sci-fi aesthetics
- overloaded dashboards or excessive text
- overly robotic imagery that feels cold or unrealistic

## Composition Notes
- Keep whitespace generous
- Make event title the strongest visual element
- Ensure date, time, and venue are easy to scan
- Use interface overlays sparingly to support the AI theme
- Keep the final visual suitable for LinkedIn and internal business communications

## Suggested Headline Treatment
Use a two-line lockup:
- **OpenShift AI for Healthcare**
- **From Ideas to Real-World Outcomes**

## CTA Treatment
Highlight: **Register now to secure your slot**
