# Brand Guidelines

Use the following brand style unless the inbox states otherwise:

## Brand Personality
- Modern
- Professional
- Friendly
- Clean
- Energetic but not overly flashy

## Tone of Voice
- Clear
- Positive
- Confident
- Easy to understand
- Short and punchy for promotional materials

## Visual Style
- Clean layout
- Strong headline
- Readable typography
- Balanced use of whitespace
- Modern event-poster feel
- Social-media-friendly visuals

## Color Direction
Primary:
- Deep blue
- White

Secondary:
- Soft cyan
- Light grey

Accent:
- Bright teal or orange for calls to action

## Design Principles
- Make the event title stand out
- Make date, time, and location easy to spot
- Avoid clutter
- Make call-to-action clear
- Keep promotional visuals suitable for professional campaigns

## Output Preference
The visual assets should look polished enough to be used for:
- event posters
- LinkedIn campaign posts
- Instagram event promotions
- internal campaign announcements
