# Campaign Brief

## Campaign Name
OpenShift AI Healthcare Innovation Workshop

## Campaign Objective
Promote an upcoming event that showcases how AI can help improve healthcare workflows, patient support, and decision assistance.

## Main Goal
Encourage healthcare professionals, IT leaders, and technical teams to register for the workshop.

## What We Want To Highlight
- Practical use of AI in healthcare
- Modern platform for deploying AI workloads
- Real-world healthcare use cases
- Value of secure and scalable AI platforms

## Desired Outcome
Create promotional materials that make the event look valuable, modern, and worth attending.
