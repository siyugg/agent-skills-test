Create two polished campaign visuals for a professional healthcare technology workshop.

PROJECT:
OpenShift AI for Healthcare: From Ideas to Real-World Outcomes

DELIVERABLE 1:
Portrait event poster, aspect ratio 4:5 or A4-friendly vertical composition.

DELIVERABLE 2:

STYLE:
Modern, professional, clean, premium corporate event campaign design. Healthcare-themed and AI-themed, but realistic and business-appropriate. Avoid childish illustration, dark sci-fi, neon cyberpunk, or clutter. The mood should feel innovative, optimistic, trustworthy, and polished.

Deep blue and white as primary colors, with soft cyan and light grey secondary tones, plus bright teal accents for call-to-action emphasis.

Environment should be bright, clean, contemporary, and professional.

COMPOSITION:
Strong headline area with clear hierarchy. Plenty of whitespace. Event details should be easy to place and read. Keep space for headline, subheadline, event details, and CTA. The design should feel suitable for LinkedIn, internal campaign use, and an event poster.

SQUARE-POST NOTES:
Use a tighter crop of the same visual language. Keep the headline short and bold, with date and CTA clearly visible in the lower third.

OUTPUT QUALITY:
High-resolution, campaign-ready, realistic, elegant, and visually balanced.
